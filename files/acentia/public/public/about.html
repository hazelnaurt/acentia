ABOUT ACENTIA

Acentia Energy is an indigenous Ghanaian oilfields support service company that provides the highest level of quality ancillary services that allow our clients to have seamless operations both onshore and offshore.

Over the years, we have built a strong network and team of industry professionals with industry experience from across the globe. In the oil and gas industry, our network and experience spans the upstream, midstream and downstream; this positions us to consistently provide our clients with safe and reliable, tailor-made support service solutions.

SOCAIL RESPONSIBILITY

We take responsibility for our collective actions, and we work hand-in-hand to contribute to the good of our customers and the community we are part of.Integrity. As a company, we set the highest standards in ethics and professionalism at all times. Our corporate relationships are built on trust, respect and transparency

OUR MISSION

Acentia Energy set out to be the most sought-after energy services contractor that satisfies and, where possible, exceeds customers’ expectations of service, through innovative application of skills and cutting edge technology. We aim to be known for our great people, topnotch customer service, and operational efficiency.
