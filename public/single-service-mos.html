Marine and offshore Services

Today’s competitive marine and offshore support industry require efficient and effective service providers that can promptly respond to clients’ needs with innovative solutions. Our marine and offshore support services team is positioned to meet today’s oilfield requirements, ensuring seamless and uninterrupted operations. Our team is a group of experts and knowledgeable people who understand the dynamics of the oil industry when it comes to manpower needs. They have occupied both managerial and field positions therefore understand the language of the job. We have extensive experience in crew management and we are always happy to provide customers with experienced and qualified marine and rig crew who are well abreast and oriented about the operations of the oil and gas industry. We have professionals from junior to senior positions, giving us the opportunity to resource any number of roles quickly, avoiding delays. Acentia Energy manages all processes, payroll, logistics and crew planning, training coordination and repatriation, allowing our customers to focus on their core business.
