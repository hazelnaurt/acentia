HEALTH, SAFETY & ENVIRONMENTAL POLICY STATEMENT

It is in the policy of ACENTIA ENERGY CO. LTD to provide safe and healthy working environment for all employees and to create an incident-free workplace, every day and everywhere. Our performance depends on our ability to continually improve the quality of the services we provide to our clients, while protecting people and minimizing the impact on the environment.

Acentia Energy Limited achieves this through;


  • Visible management commitment and accountability to HSE policy and programs of our employees, clients and interested parties.
  • Measurable objectives and programs that promote continual improvement towards ZERO incidences and the prevention of pollution.
  • Effective communication and continual maintenance of Health, Safety, Environment and Welfare programs designed to train our employees to follow safety practices, environmental protection and to recognize and correct unsafe working conditions of our employees and the community to ensure maximum satisfaction.
  • Compliance with applicable legislation and other requirements.
  • Investigate all incidents/accidents, pollution, customer complaints which may have impact on our business activities, determine the cause and amend procedures, policies and work systems if required.
  • Ensure our Health, Safety, and Environmental policy has equal importance with Acentia Energy's other policies in providing the best and most productive services in the industry.
  • We are committed to comply (ing) with our clients and customers' requirements, relevant industry standards and statutory requirements through the establishment, implementation and periodic reviews for continual improvement of our HSE Management System.
