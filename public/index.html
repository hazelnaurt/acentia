ABOUT ACENTIA

Acentia Energy is an indigenous Ghanaian oilfields support service company that provides the highest level of quality ancillary services that allow our clients to have seamless operations both onshore and offshore.

VISION

Acentia Energy set out to be the most sought-after energy services contractor that satisfies and, where possible, exceeds customers’ expectations of service, through innovative application of skills and cutting edge technology. We aim to be known for our great people, topnotch customer service, and operational efficiency.

MISION

Our mission is to build sustainable partnerships across the continent and consistently provide safe and reliable support services to our clients to enable them to achieve their project outcomes while satisfying all relevant stakeholders.
